Traffic And Revenue Momentum

The cleanest story is the same for both sites: compare December to May for traffic, revenue and keyword growth. Ducati had the sharper traffic and keyword lift, while Harley had the larger traffic base and stronger revenue growth.

Ducati traffic 1,621 to 2,887

Organic sessions from December to May, up 78.1%.

Harley traffic 7,651 to 9,221

Organic sessions from December to May, up 20.5%.

What this means: Ducati visibility and traffic improved strongly, although May revenue was below December and June was already recovering by 17 June. Harley grew from a much larger base, with both traffic and revenue higher in May than December. The next roadmap should keep building pages that can turn those traffic and keyword gains into more product discovery and sales.

The Story So Far

Priority content has already been added across Ducati apparel, parts and ownership pages, plus Harley apparel, accessories, electrical, performance and riding gear pages. The next six months should focus on new pages rather than rewriting work that has already been completed.

The monthly plan below only includes pages that have not already had dedicated content work. Completed pages can still receive small improvements as part of regular maintenance, but they are not counted as new roadmap pages.
