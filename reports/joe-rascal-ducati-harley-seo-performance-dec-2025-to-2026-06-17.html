Executive Summary

Ducati momentum is building

Ducati finished May materially ahead of December on organic sessions, Search Console clicks, visibility, top-10 rankings and average position. June 1-17 is already strong commercially, with month-to-date organic revenue above every complete month except March.

Harley remains the larger organic base

Harley delivered 52,899 organic sessions and A$93,511 in organic revenue across December-May. The site remains the larger organic engine, although keyword visibility and average position softened over the same period.

Implementation queue is clear

Both brands have completed reporting, collection content and dynamic collection schema work. The next implementation focus is product schema, homepage/title work, and selected collection/product clean-up.

Organic Shopping is material

The approved May report snapshots include GA4 Organic Shopping channel rows for December-May. Harley is the standout, with A$35,753 from Organic Shopping across the complete-month window; Ducati contributed A$743 from the same channel.
